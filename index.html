About Us – Latte Fresco

About Us – Latte Fresco

At Latte Fresco, we believe purity is a promise—not a luxury.

Founded with a deep respect for nature and tradition, Latte Fresco is more than just a dairy brand—it's a movement toward clean, honest, and wholesome living. We bring you farm-fresh, unprocessed, and chemical-free dairy products, directly from our farms to your doorstep.

🐄 From Our Family to Yours

We treat our cows and buffaloes like family. No hormones. No antibiotics. Just love, care, and natural nutrition. Our animals are raised in a stress-free, cruelty-free environment, ensuring their well-being and the superior quality of the milk they produce.

🥛 100% Pure, Unpasteurized Milk

Our milk is unpasteurized, unprocessed, and free from any adulterants—just the way nature intended. It’s delivered fresh every day from our farm to your home without the use of preservatives, chemicals, or artificial additives.

🧈 Traditional Goodness, Preserved

All our dairy products, including our artisanal ghee, are made using age-old, time-honored methods. We follow traditional bilona churning techniques to ensure our ghee retains its natural aroma, flavor, and nutritional richness.

🧼 Hygiene Meets Heritage

While we are rooted in traditional practices, we uphold the highest standards of hygiene at every stage—right from milking to packaging. Our facilities are clean, safe, and monitored, ensuring that what reaches your table is nothing but the best.

🌱 Why Choose Latte Fresco?

  • No antibiotics, hormones, or synthetic enhancers
  • Fresh, raw milk delivered directly from our farm
  • Ethically raised animals in a humane environment
  • 100% natural and traditionally prepared dairy products
  • Transparent practices you can trust

    • We’re on a mission to bring back the original taste and trust of dairy, the way it used to be.
